Introduction

Recently, lots of learning methods have attempted to convert 2-D images into 3-D shapes. The developed approaches involve two categories: model-based and model-free. The paper with title 'H3D-Net: Few-Shot High-Fidelity 3D Head Reconstruction' aims to reconstruct 3-D head surfaces, combining the advantages of model-based and model-free approaches. The way to do this is the addition of prior knowledge to the Implicit Differentiable Renderer denoted by IDR S
